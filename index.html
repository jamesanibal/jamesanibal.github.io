ABOUT

I am a DPhil student and Marie Skłodowska Curie Fellow in the University of Oxford's Computational Health Informatics Lab. I have previously completed a B.A.Sc. in Engineering Physics (UBC, 2019) and a M.Sc. in Bioinformatics (UBC, 2020). I am fascinated by the possibilities and promises of tomorrow’s technological advances, and believe that the next great discoveries will be found at the intersection of big data, machine learning, and healthcare. In particular, I am interested in designing and implementing computational models that enable personalized medicine approaches and improve patient care.
